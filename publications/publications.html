Publications

  1. Detection of the chronic kidney disease using XGBoost classifier and explaining the influence of the attributes on the model using SHAP. Raihan, M.J., Khan, M.AM., Kee, SH. et al. Sci Rep.

    Developed an interpretable CKD diagnostic system based on extensive research, featuring attribute prioritization for accurate patient classification. The system employs SHAP for interpretability and utilizes BBO-driven feature selection. This model can assist making accurate diagnoses, enabling more informed and precise medical decisions.

  2. Malaria cell image classification by explainable artificial intelligence. Md. J. Raihan and A.-A. Nahid. Health Technol.

    A malaria detection framework employing wavelet packet 2D, CNN, and Whale Optimization Algorithm. The integration of Shapley values provided critical insights into feature importance and model interpretation, enhancing diagnostic decision-making and reducing feature count by almost half using WOA.

  3. SMART HUMAN FOLLOWING BABY STROLLER USING COMPUTER VISION. M. J. Raihan, M. T. Hasan, and A.-A. Nahid. Khulna University Studies.

    I designed an innovative solution addressing the challenges of carrying baby strollers manually, by leveraging computer vision, human pose interpretation, and AI. It's an Autonomous Human-Following Baby Carrier Car that aids elderly, disabled, and pregnant individuals.

  4. Breast cancer classification along with feature prioritization using machine learning algorithms. A. A. Nahid, Md. J. Raihan, and A. A.-M. Bulbul. Health Technol.

    Conducted groundbreaking research employing state-of-the-art Machine Learning techniques to classify Breast Cancer patients with 82.85% accuracy, revealing Glucose as the pivotal parameter for detection. We demonstrated expertise in feature selection, SHAP interpretation, and the traditional Borda method for comprehensive analysis.

  5. Md. J. Raihan and A.-A. Nahid, Classification of histopathological colon cancer images using particle swarm optimization-based feature selection algorithm, in Diagnostic Biomedical Signal and Image Processing Applications with Deep Learning Methods, K. Polat and S. Öztürk, Eds., in Intelligent ata-Centric Systems (Book).

    Developed a Computer-Aided Diagnosis (CAD) system for Colon Cancer (CC) utilizing PSO, CNN, wavelet, and SHAP, achieving an outstanding 99.73% accuracy. This innovation reduces time complexity for prediction, enhances diagnostic transparency, and ensures precision in CC detection, revolutionizing healthcare.

  6. A.-A. Nahid, J. Raihan, N. Sikder, and S. R. Sabuj, Histopathological breast cancer image classification with feature prioritization using a heuristic algorithm, in Artificial Intelligence in Cancer Diagnosis and Prognosis, Volume 2: Breast and bladder cancer (Book).

    Developed a Computer-Aided Diagnosis (CAD) system for Breast Cancer (BC) detection, surpassing manual biopsy limitations. Leveraged Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) and innovative feature selection techniques, achieving an impressive 91.92% accuracy and 96.90% precision on the BreakHis dataset.
