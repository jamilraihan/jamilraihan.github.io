Autistic Kids Companion: An Automatic Exercise Assessment and Companion for Autistic Kids and Motor Dysfunction Patients Using ML

Md. Johir Raihan | 25 Sept 2023 | © 2023 Md. Johir Raihan

Title: Autistic Kids Companion: An Automatic Exercise Assessment and Companion for Autistic Kids and Motor Dysfunction Patients Using ML
Authors: Md. J. Raihan
Achivement: 1st position (Champion) Award

Abstract

Many people with Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) have trouble with their ability to coordinate their movements and process their senses. They may also display repetitive or stereotyped motions. However, regular physical activity and exercise can have many advantages for people with ASD, such as better physical health, and decreased anxiety. I have proposed an app idea that will act as their companion and make their exercise more enjoyable. The app titled “Autistic Kids Companion” uses a Mediapipe-based pose landmark algorithm to extract landmarks which are given to a Machine Learning (ML) model to predict the score of the performed exercise and analyze their body movement. The app will also provide which body part they should focus more on to accurately do the exercise. I have also provided an idea to use animated characters in place of their body so that it is more entertaining for them. Also, I have added a page that will contain many training exercises to guide their parents. The app will also collect statistical data about their child's exercise progress and will show a progress chart. Further, I have implemented the idea of uploading their performed exercise to an online server, that will automatically train a model on the new dataset and distribute the new model to the mobile app. In short, the application will enable families with ASD children to live a better and healthier life.
